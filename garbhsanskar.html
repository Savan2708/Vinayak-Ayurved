Garbhsanskar : The science to get the child of desired Attributes.

According to Ayurveda, a unique concept was developed by ancient Rishis through which would-be parents get an opportunity of - "pregnancy by planning and not by chance " due to which the parents can have a baby with all the psycho physiological attributes.
This means parents can plan and program their baby the way they want ! The wonderful ,reliable ,effective and unique science catering to the needs of modern parents.

• In this modern era, every parent dreams of a child who achieves excellence. And GARBHSANSKAR will help parents to fulfill this criteria of having talented, free-willed, powerful children.
• Garbhsanskar helps to avoid hereditary transmitted disorders from parents to their children.
• Garbhsanskar helps to incorporate desired attributes in your child and tries to stop unwanted attributes in your child.
